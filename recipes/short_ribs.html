Short Ribs

Camping was a lot of fun! But let's be real, one of the best parts of camping is, "what's for dinner??". Now unless you want to be

one of those people staring into the fire at night hungry, you better listen up because we are making short ribs in this section. What is

short rib you ask? Probably one of the best things you could ever have while camping because cooking it as SUPER easy and feeds a lot of people. Okay,

not exactly super easy as putting it in water or an air fryer, you still have some work you gotta put it, BUT it's ALL WORTH IT!! Without further ado,

lets get into the ingredients and then the steps!

Ingredients

  • Short ribs (Obviously)
  • Mustard (it's just what we used)
  • Some meat seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • BBQ sauce **IMPORTANT**
  • Someone who can start a fire
  • A KNIFE
  • The determination to WIN
  • Beer **IMPORTANT**

Steps

  1. Skim all that silver skin off the short ribs **ACTUALLY IMPORTANT**
  2. Slather that bitch in mustard
  3. Ok wait maybe season it first with that seasoning
  4. Also put some BBQ sauce on tha bitch
  5. Drink some beer **IMPORTANT**
  6. DRINK MORE BEEER **VERY IMPORTANT**
  7. Start the fire (We didn't start it though)
  8. Oh yeah I forgot to tell you to get tin foil lmaooo
  9. AFter you get the tin foil, cover it
  10. Let the fire create some coals n shit
  11. Now put the ribs in the hot ashes and try to not place it so close to the fire
  12. After like 4 hours of this slow cooking take it out and check on it
  13. If it didn't rain like a mfer the whole time, it should be ready to eat!
  14. Now enjoy!!
