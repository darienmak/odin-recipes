Hot Pockets

Hello my friend! Here's another amazing and popular university/college meal that tastes great and can be filling!

Now compared to cup noodles, this is a much easier lunch/dinner to make for yourself.

It's really just as easy as taking it out of the packet and throwing it into a microwave and go fack off for a few minutes,

and it's done just like christmas dinner! But in this recipe, we'll be exploring the use of an air fryer instead which will be

probably 20x better than a microwave. Now without further ado, let's get into the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Any brand of hot pocket
  • Literally any air fryer that hasn't been washed for 20 days minimum
  • Water (Stay hydrated)
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 4 cups of air

Steps

  1. Get the hot pockets from Walmart
  2. Catch the next train home from Walmart
  3. Wait on the train until you get home
  4. Get home (NICE!!!!)
  5. Drink some water **IMPORTANT**
  6. Get hot pocket out of your grocery bag
  7. Pour 1/2 cup of milk on the ground
  8. Follow that up with 1/4 cup of sugar and 4 cups of air
  9. Drink water **IMPORTANT**
  10. Get your seasoned air fryer and plug it in
  11. Put hot pocket in da basket
  12. CRANK THAT BITCH TO THE HIGHEST TEMP
  13. PUT THAT HOT POCKET IN THERE
  14. Wait for like iunno 5 minutes?
  15. Ok take it ou- YO YO IT'S HOT **IMPORTANT**
  16. Drink some water **VERY IMPORTANT**
  17. Wait for the hot pocket to cool down on the ground
  18. Okay now pick it up and ENJOY!!! **IMPORTANT**
