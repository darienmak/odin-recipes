Cup Noodle

Cup noodles are the staple for every college/university student all across the world. It is incredibly affordable and always provides you with a full belly

and delcicious experience! Here's the catch though, although it is as simple as just add water and wait for roughly 10 minutes for it to cook, there are

many more creative ways to enjoy this amazing meal. It's as simple as adding some protein and more vegetables into the cup as it cooks to really add some

more extra depth to your cup noodle meals. So now I present to you my absolute favourite recipe, the cooked sausage and spinach cup noodle recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cup noodle (Any one you want)
  • Cooked sausages from Walmart
  • Some spinach from Walmart as well
  • You will need a kettle
  • Water (obviously)
  • A determined heart

Steps

  1. Purchase cup noodle from Walmart
  2. Take the bus back home
  3. Realize you forgot to purchase the sausage and spinaches too in step 1
  4. Get back on the bus to Walmart
  5. Now go home
  6. Boil up some water
  7. Wait for it to boil
  8. Ok it's done boiling
  9. Open the cup noodle and add all the given packets **VERY IMPORTANT**
  10. Now slap 2 sauasages in there or however many can fit
  11. Don't forget the greens, add some spinach on that bitch
  12. Ok NOW add the water and be careful not to burn yourself
  13. Go play some CS2 or something, come back in 10 minutes
  14. Now get a fork or chopsticks, open up that cup noodle and slurp it up
  15. Now you also got extra protein and veggies to go with it wow so yummy!
  16. Enjoy, or DIE
